Today we're launching the Divest for AR Future website — a new resource for Arkansans who want to understand how their pension funds are being invested and what they can do about it.

## Who we are

Divest for AR Future is a grassroots campaign made up of Arkansas educators, public employees, and concerned citizens. We believe that pension fund investment decisions should be made on the basis of sound financial analysis — not outside pressure.

## What we've found

Over the past year, our research team has filed Freedom of Information Act requests with four Arkansas state agencies: the State Treasury, ATRS, APERS, and the Auditor of State. We've obtained and analyzed **957 public records**.

What those records reveal is troubling:

- **$155 million** in Arkansas pension funds have been committed to Israel Bonds
- **All three major credit agencies** downgraded Israel's credit rating
- **An internal Treasury memo** recommended against new purchases
- **Zero independent credit analyses** were found in the entire document record
- **The ATRS Board Chair voted no**, warning that the process departed from standard practice

## What's next

This website is the beginning. In the coming weeks and months, we'll be sharing detailed findings, providing resources for educators and public employees, and building a coalition of Arkansans who believe their retirement savings deserve better stewardship.
