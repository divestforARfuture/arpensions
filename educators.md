## What's happening with YOUR pension

In June 2025, the Arkansas Teacher Retirement System (ATRS) Board of Trustees authorized up to **$50 million** in Israel Bonds purchases — direct loans to the Israeli government that cannot be sold before maturity.

This authorization came after all three major credit agencies downgraded Israel's credit rating, and without an independent credit analysis of the investment.

## Why it matters

- **Your retirement security is at stake.** ATRS manages pensions for over 100,000 current and retired Arkansas educators.
- **The Board Chair objected.** Danny Knight voted against the authorization, warning that the process departed from normal investment practice.
- **No independent analysis was performed.** Standard fiduciary practice requires pension funds to conduct their own due diligence — not rely on sales materials from the bond issuer.
